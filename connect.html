Looking for a team? Recruiting for your team? Either way, you are in the right place

TEAMBUILDER GUIDE

If you're looking for a team:

1. Go to the registration form and select "Looking for a team".
2. Set the response to "Open to Teammates" as "Yes"
3. Your info should now be visible to other participants in the "Individuals" tab
4. Wait for the team leaders to contact you (or) Reach out to the team leaders in the z that you like to express your interest!
5. When you join a team, edit the registration form and set the response "Open to Teammates" as "No"

  • Find Teams

If you're a team leader:

1. Go to the registration form and select "Registering for my team".
2. Set your status as "Open to Teammates"
3. Your info is now public to other participants in the "Teams" tab
4. Browse the "Individuals" tab to find Teammates. Call/mail them up
5. When your team is full, edit the registration form and set the response for "Open to Teammates" as "No"
